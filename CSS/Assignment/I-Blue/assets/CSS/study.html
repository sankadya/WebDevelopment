Overview Why TTBS? Features FAQs

When customers want to connect with your business, they expect it to be simple, quick, and free. The TTBS 1800 number, combined with WABA Voice API calling, removes cost barriers and provides customers with a simple, trusted way to reach you anytime, from anywhere in the world (available only in regions where WhatsApp calling is supported).

For businesses, it unlocks more inbound opportunities, stronger engagement, and valuable insights from every interaction. With smart routing, advanced IVR, and branded welcome prompts, you can deliver a seamless customer experience.

Features like call reporting, blacklisting/whitelisting for cost control, and a secure iManage portal ensure you never miss a call while keeping operations efficient and data protected.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is a domestic Toll-Free Number?

A dedicated number that allows customers to call your business free of charge, with the enterprise bearing the cost.

2. How does it benefit my business?

It encourages more customer calls, improves brand accessibility, and makes your business always reachable.

3.Can I route calls to multiple locations or numbers?

Yes. Calls can be routed by geography, time, load balancing, or directed to up to 20 mobile numbers.

4.Is the service secure?

Yes. All call data is secured with TTBS in-house iManage portal, ensuring complete privacy and control.

5. What kind of businesses should use TFN 1800?

Any customer-facing business, be it retail, e-commerce, healthcare, BFSI, logistics, or services, that want to make inbound communication simple and cost-free.
